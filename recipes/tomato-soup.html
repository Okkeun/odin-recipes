Tomato soup with pasta

This recipe is not mine. Original source of photo and recipe belongs to:

Kuchnia Ani

Ingredients

  • carrots - 330 g
  • 1 parsley - 85 g
  • 1/4 celery - 130
  • 1 small onion - 70 g
  • 1 leek - light part
  • 1500 ml of broth
  • salt, pepper, lovage
  • 200 g tomato paste
  • 80 ml of sweet cream 12% or 18%
  • 1 bay leaf, 3 balls of allspice
  • a teaspoon of butter - 8 g

Preparation

Peel the Italian vegetables. In a pot on a teaspoon of butter I fry a leek cut lengthwise in half. When the leek glazes slightly, I add the peeled vegetables (whole): carrot, celery, parsley, onion. I add leaves and allspice. I fry the vegetables for a minute, then pour the broth - 1500 ml.

Tip. If you want it is a good time to add 4 chicken drumsticks or 1 large chicken quarter. I cook the soup over medium heat until the vegetables are tender (and the meat is ready, if you chose to add it).

At the end of cooking, I add tomato paste, cook for a while. I pour in 80 ml of 18% cream. You can replace it with 12%, 30% cream or sour cream, remembering to temper it beforehand. I season the soup to taste with salt, pepper, a pinch of dried lovage.

Tip. If you want the soup you can strain the vegetables through a sieve or fish them out with a straining spoon. I always do it this way.

Slice the carrots into slices and add them back to the soup. I serve the tomato soup with short noodles garnished with chopped parsley or dill
