Tomato soup with pasta

This recipe is not mine. Original source of photo and recipe belongs to:

Kuchnia Ani

Ingredients

  • carrots - 330 g
  • 1 parsley - 85 g
  • 1/4 celery - 130
  • 1 small onion - 70 g
  • 1 leek - light part
  • 1500 ml of broth
  • salt, pepper, lovage
  • 200 g tomato paste
  • 80 ml of sweet cream 12% or 18%
  • 1 bay leaf, 3 balls of allspice
  • a teaspoon of butter - 8 g
